What are we about?

What is the Story Edifice? You may know what our motto is--A Kid's Roleplaying Dream--but to truly understand this site, we must first learn what roleplaying is.

So... What is roleplay? Roleplay is a game where people impersonate different characters, whether a sage elf or a pesky nymph. Simple! Sometimes, roleplay MODs (or moderators) choose the setting and or decide how actions will generally go. Roleplay can be either live-action (LARP) or written (CTS).

Note: Just in case you don't know what edifice means, I provided the definition below.

Edifice: an abstract structure of ideas and beliefs (can be literal or figurative)
