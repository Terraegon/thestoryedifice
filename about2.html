What is roleplay? /h1>

What is roleplay? Roleplay is a game where people impersonate different characters, whether a sage elf or a pesky nymph. Simple! Sometimes, roleplay MODs (or moderators) choose the setting and or decide how actions will generally go. Roleplay can be either live-action (LARP) or written (CTS). Simple! To learn more information about roleplaying and its rules, you can click on the How to Roleplay tab.
